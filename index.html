Game Master Guide

Follow these steps to guide the game:

  • Start the Meeting Period (Night Phase):
    • Everyone closes their eyes.
    • Web and Graphic Designer: Open your eyes and recognize each other as linked. If one is fired, the other will also be fired.
    • Developer: Open your eyes and select a person to protect. This person will be immune from being fired. You cannot protect the same person twice in a row.
    • Hackers: Open your eyes and silently point to one person to fire.
    • Competitor: Open your eyes and silently point to one person to fire (independent of the Hackers).
    • HR: Open your eyes and choose to:
      • Save a player targeted by the Hackers.
      • Fire a player of your choice (use this power only once).
    • Influencer: Open your eyes and select a player to automatically receive one vote against them during the day phase.
    • Everyone closes their eyes again.
  • Move to the Debriefing Period (Day Phase):
    • Everyone opens their eyes.
    • Announce the results of the night:
      • Who was protected by the Developer or HR.
      • Who (if anyone) was fired by the Hackers or Competitor.
    • Generate a random event using the QR code and explain its effect to the players.
    • Facilitate a debate where players discuss and try to identify the Hackers or Competitor.
    • Host a vote:
      • Players vote on who they think should be fired.
      • The Manager’s vote counts double. In the event of a tie, the Manager decides who is fired.
    • Reveal the fired player’s role and remove them from the game.
  • Repeat the steps until a victory condition is met:
    • Hackers win if their number equals or exceeds the remaining players.
    • The Competitor wins if they eliminate everyone else.
    • Employees win if all Hackers and the Competitor are fired.

Good luck, Game Master! Keep the agency safe!
